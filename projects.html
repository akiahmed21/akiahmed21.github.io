HangarPRO VR

HangarPRO VR is an immersive VR training simulation powered by Cognitive 3D, designed to enhance pre-flight safety and reliability. This groundbreaking tool equips maintenance teams with the ability to detect micro-tears on aircraft exteriors using the innovative Visual Interaction Analysis (VIA) methodology. By utilizing advanced eye-tracking analytics, it uncovers critical insights within complex datasets, ensuring safer skies for everyone. HangarPro VR won second place in AeroSpatial Exploration in MIT Reality Hack 2025.
